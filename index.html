what tono does

four ways tono has your back

tap the coach button, pick a tone, and tono rewrites your message on the spot.

warmer

soften sharp edges. add warmth without losing your point — perfect for saying no gracefully.

clearer

cut the fluff. make your point land in one read — no more back-and-forth clarification.

funnier

lighten the mood without trying too hard. a touch of wit when it earns its keep.

safer

flag risky phrasing before it lands — typos aside, the things you actually wish you could unsend.

how it works

three taps. that's it.

tono lives inside your keyboard — no new app to open, no context switch.

1

install the keyboard

download tono from the app store, add it in iOS Settings, and grant Full Access.

2

type as usual

use tono in any app — messages, mail, slack, notes. switch with the globe key like any keyboard.

3

tap coach

hit the tono button, pick warmer / clearer / funnier / safer, and a rewrite drops in instantly.
